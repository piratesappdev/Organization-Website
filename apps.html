PirateRate

A majority of students in college wish that they knew more about campus earlier on in their college journey. Examples of these things are class difficulty, personality of professors, kinds of food, the best events, & courses. PirateRate is an application that creates a space where current students at Hampton will not feel as if they have to find everything out for themselves. PirateRate makes it so that students never have to run the risk of taking a class with a professor whose teaching style they would not prefer, going to a boring/unpopular event, paying for low quality food/services, living in poorly rated housing, and more.
